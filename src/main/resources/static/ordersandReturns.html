Return Policy

General Return Guidelines

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for most items.

Returnable Items

  • Unopened raw materials in original packaging
  • Unused medical instruments with all accessories
  • Defective or damaged products
  • Items that don't match the description

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom or personalized products
  • Opened chemical substances
